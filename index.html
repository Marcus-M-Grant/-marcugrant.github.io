About

Background & Expertise

Marcus Grant is a Director of Analytics at Open Loot Studios, where he leads data-driven strategy across a portfolio of eight games generating over $170M in revenue. He specializes in applying AI, advanced analytics, and experimentation to drive user growth, monetization, and operational efficiency, working closely with C-suite leadership and engineering teams.

Prior to Open Loot, Marcus built machine learning and financial modeling solutions at Verizon and Mercor AI, focusing on optimizing large-scale business decisions. He holds an MBA from the Wharton School, where he was a Palmer Scholar (top 5%), and a BA from Yale University.

Before his business career, Marcus served as an Infantry Officer in the U.S. Army, earning a Bronze Star and ranking as the top junior officer in a special operations unit. He combines a background in leadership, analytics, and operations to drive high-impact business outcomes in complex environments.
